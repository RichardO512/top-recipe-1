Chicken and Spinach Alfredo Lasagna

Description:

This easy white chicken lasagna with spinach is a different, yet delicious twist on a classic lasagna

Ingredients:

Steps:
  1. 1 (8 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  2. 3 cups heavy cream
  3. 2 (10.5 ounce) cans condensed cream of mushroom soup
  4. 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  5. ¼ cup butter
